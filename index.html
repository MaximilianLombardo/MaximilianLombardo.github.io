Professional Background

My path began with academic research in biotechnology and experimental therapeutics, where I honed my skills in genomics and statistical analysis. Moving from academia into industry, I expanded my expertise in machine learning and data-driven discovery. During an international research internship, I delved into advanced modeling techniques, focusing on multi-omics data and disease phenotyping.

Afterward, I joined a biotech startup environment, applying graph-based clustering and dimensionality reduction methods to characterize and visualize single-cell data. Collaborating closely with cross-functional teams, I contributed to strategic partnerships and milestone achievements in drug discovery.

My passion for product development crystallized as I transitioned into a senior technical product role, driving the roadmap for large-scale open-source tools in single-cell and spatial genomics. Working with engineers, UX specialists, and scientists, I developed and shipped AI-driven features that significantly reduced data annotation bottlenecks.

I also dedicated efforts to building out an AI platform strategy, scoping MVP requirements for model development and data processing pipelines. This blend of product vision and technical expertise continues to fuel my motivation to create user-centric, cutting-edge solutions for the life sciences community.

Alongside my industry roles, I have explored entrepreneurship, co-founding outreach programs for high-tech ventures and conceptualizing SaaS products in the regenerative medicine sphere. My overarching goal is to champion innovation that advances both scientific research and real-world impact.
