About Me

I am a computational biologist and data scientist with experience applying machine learning and algorithmic methods to biomedical research. My work focuses on developing scalable pipelines for single-cell analysis, using advanced deep learning approaches to explore complex biological datasets.

Beyond my work in computational biology, I am an avid creative coder who enjoys building generative art installations and interactive visual experiences. My interest in creative coding stems from a fascination with the intersection of aesthetics and algorithms—where pattern, form, and function converge to produce new forms of artistic expression.

  • Computational Biology: Single-cell RNA-seq, High-throughput data processing, Genomic Data Integration
  • Creative Coding: Generative Art (Processing, p5.js), Interactive Visuals (Three.js), Data-Driven Installations
